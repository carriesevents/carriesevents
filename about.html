Carrie’s Events

Carries events is an events business we offer our services to make your event truly magical, we have a moongate arch, large candy cart, small candy cart, Snack bar, Donut wall, welcome mirror, flower wall, treat stand, we can make your favours & we have more to come.
We also make hampers, sweet & chocolate boxes & many more goodies for the seasonal gifts throughout the year.
Carrie’s Events started in June 2019 and we have grew since, we would love to be a part of your big day or event & would love to hear from you.
